What is DIMA?

DIMA is a placeholder identification product for construction conduit that replaces current products with time and money saved on installation and with the same integrity. Currently, there is not a form to show customers how much time and money they are saving if they switch from current products to DIMA.

DIMAS's Savings Calculator

DIMA's Savings Calculator, calculates the amount the customer saves by using the new placeholder. The user enters the job number, the pipe size, and the total number of holes per job. The calculator will display the difference between the money spent on current practices with the money that is spent on the new placeholder.
